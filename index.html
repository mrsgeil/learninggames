💡 Speed Tips:

  • 0 & 1 facts: Instant! Zero makes everything zero, one keeps it the same
  • 2 facts: Just double the number (2×7 = 7+7 = 14)
  • 5 facts: Always end in 0 or 5 (5×6 = 30, 5×7 = 35)
  • 10 facts: Add a zero to the end (10×8 = 80)
  • 9 facts: The digits add up to 9 (9×4 = 36, 3+6 = 9)
  • Practice daily: Even 5 minutes helps build speed!

🎯 Challenge Yourself:

Start with easier levels (0, 1, 2, 5, 10) to build confidence and speed. Once you can do 20+ questions per minute on these, move to harder facts like 6, 7, 8, 9. The mixed levels will really test your mastery!
